I've Lost My Voice

I’ve lost my voice
Have you heard it?

A scream in silence
Not always heard
A mumbling shyness
Lost of words

I’ve lost my voice
Have you heard it?

A noise in the background
Jitter of a voice
A spirit that drowned
Not by choice

I’ve lost my voice
Have you heard it?

The laughs, the cries
The voice once said
A blanket of lies
Filled with dread

I’ve lost my voice
Have you heard it?

The songs it sang
On a note so high
The words that rang
In the rainbow sky

I’ve lost my voice
Have you heard it?

My voice, speak again
Speak for no one but you
In rains, sing again
Don’t think listens who

Have you heard it?
