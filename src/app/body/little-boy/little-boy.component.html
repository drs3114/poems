Little Boy

A tiny little boy
With an innocent smile
Walks with joy
On a bone filled aisle

The boy lies still
Very safely underground
Don’t move. Hold still
Let his demon come around

His eyes are open
Close it please
He sees no weapon
But his breath will cease

Ancient Gods beyond the hill
Show no mercy – not their will
The little boy is hungry
Kill him please

The gods are singing
The devils are the choir
Make no mistake
The worlds on fire

Little boy with little voice
Where did you go?
You have no choice
Your casket will glow
