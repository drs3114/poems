The Raging Conch

The voices in my head
Screaming inside
A curse to fathom
While no one beside

Don’t label me
Don’t name me
I am nobody
Nobody knows me

Disrespect and dismay
True cultural heritage
A word if I may
My song my stage

Dark spells, land mines
And the land of ecstasy
Dragons breath fire
And cleanse my agony

Few words not more
Don’t scare me anymore
Wild sky winds roar
The sun doesn’t rise anymore
