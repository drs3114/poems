Unknown and Untold

The days are not bright enough
For words to travel afar
But the words, seem all sold
From sources that never told

Silent whispers speak aloud
It’s now in the midst of crowd
Lay the carpets. Paint the roads
Darling lie visits the town

He told she. She told he
Who? We’ll never know
Unbeknownst, a silent he
Buried in deep black snow

Hear the truth. Hear you all
The truth that never spoke
Hear me speak. Hear me now
The wrecked silence broke

Crimson red, freely spread
Painted the roads of town
He went blind on his own
In darkness, he lay unknown
