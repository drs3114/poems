A Forgotten Grave

A forgotten grave
On a lonely road
A man lies brave
Sleeping deep underground

Is he still alive?
Does he still breath?
Is he dreaming of life?
While his brothers weep

What does he look like?
My mirror, do you know?
What did he speak like?
My voice, do you know?

No flowers on the grave
No footsteps to follow.
Who lies there?
No one can know
