Ghost

Sometimes I see a ghost
Creeping behind me
Still still and waiting
Scheming behind me

A ghost - my forgotten
I know where you stand
I stood too, unknown
my shadow, strangely behind

A time dare once spoke
Brave brave and aloud
Tick tock and minutes fell
Like dominoes of memories old

Chime in winds, ringing slow
A melody somewhere hidden
A ghost of time singing low
A carol somewhere ridden
