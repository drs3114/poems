Shadow

In the shadow lay my dreams
My eyes, my heart wants to see.
Chasing down that shadows path,
I do not know what I see.

There is a light above me
Its colors so bright and vibrant.
Yet the shadow that I see,
Seems dark and follows me.

The music, the dance, the rhythm
Oh, so beautiful things!
Yet the shadow that follows me,
Seem to hide behind me.

How should I know my dreams?
How does it know me?
I do not know what I dream
I will just wait for it to see me.
