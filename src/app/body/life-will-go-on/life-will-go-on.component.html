Life Will Go On

Life will go on
Everyone moves on
In the test of time,
You too shall go on

Leather boots and bowtie
Dressed fair to tell a lie
Extraordinarily pleased
Wonderfully deceived

Centerpiece with flower vase
Chandelier lights the maze
Anointed holds the free
Officiated with a decree

Testaments behold
A truth never told
Laws and prejudice
Of unknown origins

Yet, life will go on
Everyone moves on
In the test of time,
You too shall go on
